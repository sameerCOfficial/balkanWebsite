Introduction

A system of tribute where Christian youth from the Balkans were forcibly converted to Islam and drafted into Ottoman service. Came from both rich and poor Balkan families and created factions of soldiers and officials loyal to the Sultan which also counterbalanced Turkish nobility who sometimes opposed the Sultan Devshirme produced many of the Ottomans provincial governors, military commanders, and divans between 1400s -1600s period. Many scholars believe devshirme as a violation of Islamic Law Enslavement of christian boys violates Dhimmi protections guarenteed in Islam Dhimmi is the term for non muslims living in Islamic lands. It applied to Christians, Jews and Sabians who are considered “People of the Book” in Islamic theology Jews and Christians were required to pay jizyah.
